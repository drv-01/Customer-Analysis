# 📊 Customer Analysis Dashboard

## 🧾 Overview
This project presents a **Customer Analysis Dashboard** built using sales data from FY 2020–21. It provides insights into revenue trends, customer demographics, geographic performance, and purchasing behavior.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to make **data-driven decisions** by exploring patterns across time, regions, age groups, gender, and discount strategies.

---

## 📁 Dataset
- **File:** `sales_06_FY2020-21 copy.csv`
- **Contents include:**
  - Order Date
  - Customer Demographics (Age, Gender)
  - Product Categories
  - Sales & Revenue
  - Discounts
  - Quantity Ordered
  - State / Region

---

## 📌 Dashboard Features

### 1. Month-wise Revenue
- Tracks revenue trends across months  
- Identifies seasonal peaks and dips  

### 2. Revenue by State
- Geographic visualization of revenue distribution  
- Highlights top-performing and underperforming states  

### 3. Age-wise Sales Analysis
- Breaks down sales across age groups  
- Helps identify target customer segments  

### 4. Region-wise Revenue Share
- Displays contribution by regions:
  - South
  - Midwest
  - West
  - Northeast

### 5. Gender-wise Sales Analysis
- Compares category-wise spending between male and female customers  

### 6. Quantity vs Discount Correlation
- Scatter plot showing relationship between:
  - Discount %
  - Quantity ordered  

---

## 💡 Key Insights

### 📈 Revenue Trends
- **December shows the highest revenue (~$57.7M)** indicating strong holiday season sales  
- Early months (Jan–Feb) have relatively lower revenue  

### 🌎 Regional Performance
- **South region leads (~38%)** in revenue contribution  
- Midwest follows (~27%), indicating strong central market performance  

### 🗺️ State-Level Insights
- States like **California and Texas** generate significant revenue  
- Some central states show moderate but consistent performance  

### 👥 Customer Demographics
- **Age group 30–40 contributes the highest revenue (~$44.8M)**  
- Very young customers (<20) contribute the least  

### 🚻 Gender Insights
- Sales distribution is relatively balanced across genders  
- Top categories for both genders:
  - Mobiles & Tablets  
  - Entertainment  
  - Appliances  

### 🛒 Product Categories
**Top-performing categories:**
- Mobiles & Tablets  
- Appliances  
- Entertainment  

**Low-performing categories:**
- Books  
- School & Education  
- Health & Sports  

### 💸 Discount Behavior
- Weak correlation between discount and quantity  
- High discounts **do not always guarantee higher sales volume**  
- Some outliers indicate bulk purchases at high discounts
